# Aqua Acrobatics Unofficial [Forge]

[![License: Unlicense](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-Unlicense-blue.svg)](http://unlicense.org/)

**An unofficial continuation of the Aqua Acrobatics mod for Minecraft 1.12.2.**

This mod is a direct backport of the new movement features added in modern versions of Minecraft, most notably the swimming mechanic. It also includes numerous related improvements and bug fixes, making the game feel more polished and up-to-date.

**This is not an official work of the original authors (Fuzs and embeddedt). Please do not report bugs to the original authors!**

---

## 📖 Features

*   **🏊 Swimming:** Triggered by sprinting underwater. It increases your speed and adds a fancy animation, making traveling through water much more enjoyable.
*   **🦎 Sneaking & Crawling:** The player's height while sneaking has been reduced to 1.5 blocks, allowing you to fit through smaller gaps. The player hitbox is forced to stay in a smaller state if there isn't enough space available, enabling you to, for example, crawl through one-block-high holes.
*   **🌊 Water & Fog Colors:** Backports the new water colors and fog from the Update Aquatic. The colors are fully customizable in the config file, and support for mods like Biomes O' Plenty is included out of the box.
*   **🎮 Additional Movement Tweaks:**
    *   Smooth eye level transitions (backport of the fix for MC-48401).
    *   Easier elytra takeoff, similar to Minecraft 1.15 and onwards.
    *   Sideways sprinting.
    *   Option to disable sprint activation by double-tapping the forward key.
    *   The air meter no longer refills instantly when you reach the surface.
*   **🐞 Bug Fixes:** Numerous bugs have been fixed, many of which were taken from the original Aqua Acrobatics GitHub repository.
